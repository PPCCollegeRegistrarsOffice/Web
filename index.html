Office of the College Registrar

The Pangasinan Polytechnic College (PPC) Registrar's Office is entrusted with maintaining the accuracy and integrity of student academic records, ensuring their availability upon request in accordance with established policies and regulations.

Mission

To deliver accurate, timely, and responsive academic support in records, registration, and enrollment—empowering students and supporting institutional excellence.

Vision

A trusted partner in academic success through efficient, secure, and student centered services.

Goals

  • Ensure accurate and secure student records.
  • Provide fast and reliable registration and enrollment services.
  • Support student success through responsive service.
  • Embrace digital solutions for efficient academic processes.
  • Uphold integrity, transparency, and professionalism.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I request a copy of my grades?
    You can fill out the request form under the "Request" section of this website. Select “Copy of Grades” as your request type.
  • How long does it take to process requests?
    Most requests are processed within 3–5 working days.
  • What are the office hours?
    Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM (excluding holidays).
  • Where is the Registrar’s Office located?
    At the PPC Building, Narciso Ramos Sports & Civic Center, Alvear St., Poblacion, Lingayen, Pangasinan.
  • Can I authorize someone to claim my documents?
    Yes, but the authorized representative must present a valid ID and an authorization letter.
  • How do I get a Certificate of Registration (COR)?
    Select "Certificate of Registration" in the Request form. Make sure your enrollment status is active.
  • What if I entered the wrong information in the form?
    Please email us immediately at ppcregistraroffice@gmail.com with your correction details.
